Under the Hood

Cutting-edge tech at every layer.

Arc Light isn't just fast software on the same old architecture. We rebuilt the stack from scratch.

Arc Consensus

Our novel BFT variant with pipelined execution — 2/3 stake for finality, zero forks, sub-second confirmation guaranteed.

Parallel EVM

Concurrent transaction execution with optimistic conflict resolution. Up to 16x throughput vs. sequential EVM implementations.

ZK Data Availability

State commitments posted with ZK validity proofs, giving cryptographic guarantees without running a full node.

Dynamic Sharding

Execution shards spin up automatically under load, then consolidate during quiet periods — you pay for exactly what you use.
